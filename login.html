About EcoSaver

EcoSaver is a gamified learning platform for Indian students that inspires eco-friendly habits and awareness through real-world challenges, quizzes, and community action. Our unique points and badge system incentivizes repeated sustainable actions ‚Äî making environmental learning fun, measurable, and impactful. We use AI-powered eco-suggestions and region-specific content to maximize local inspiration and measurable green impact.

How It Works

Platform Flow

  • Users join, personalize profile, and start tackling local eco-challenges
  • Eco actions logged, verified (manual/proof/image), and rewarded with Eco-Points and badges
  • AI module serves regionally relevant eco-tips and personalized suggestions
  • Leaderboards and community pages foster friendly competition and teamwork
Data privacy, security, and scalability are core to our design.

Key Features

  • Gamified eco-habit tracker
  • Real-time leaderboard
  • AI-powered image verification for task proof
  • Educational library with quizzes and resources
  • Community engagement and challenges
  • Persistent ecoPoints and badge system
